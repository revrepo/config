Domain Control Verification - Email Verification

The Email Verification method will require you to select an email address which can be used by our SSL certification authority GlobalSign CA to send a domain control verification email. 

The email message will be addressed from GlobalSign CA (our SSL certification authority) and will ask you to confirm that the domain can be added to Rev Software, Inc.’s CloudSSL certificate.

Please click on the included in the email confirmation link to complete the verification process.

Please note that you can repeat the verification procedure at any time later by using “Verify Domain Control” action icon () in the list of managed SSL names.
