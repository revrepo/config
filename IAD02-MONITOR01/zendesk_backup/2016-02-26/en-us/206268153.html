How to Get a User Profile

To get the profile of a user, use the object_id and the same endpoint with a GET request.

https://api.revapm.net/v1/users/55bff4b8c01a42a2348170f7

cURL example:

curl -u 'user@example.com:password' -H 'Content-Type: application/json' http://api.revapm.net/v1/users/55bff4b8c01a42a2348170f7
