Domain Control Verification - DNS Verification

The DNS Verification method will require you to add a new TXT record to the domain name. Our system will provide the TXT record value via a "DNS Verification" pop-up that will be displayed after you add a new SSL name.

You can find instructions on how to add a TXT record to your domain host's DNS records by visiting the help center of your domain registrar.

GoDaddy  Namecheap  Bluehost  HostGator  DreamHost  Name.com  Hover  Gandi.net  1&1  Web.com                                                                                                                              

Please note that you can repeat the verification procedure at any time later by using “Verify Domain Control” action icon () in the list of managed SSL names.
